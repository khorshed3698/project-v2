Occupancy Certificate User Guide
In any place under the jurisdiction of RAJUK, if any person or public or private organization
wants to construct a new building or infrastructure, he has to take a construction permit.
Occupancy Certificate is required for notification and fit-for-use certificate after
completion of construction as per DAP design.
The applicant can directly apply for the Occupancy Certificate application. The applicant can
fill the Occupancy Certificate form by clicking on Occupancy Certificate on the left side
menu bar. Click on the + New Occupancy Certificate button to apply anew.
After approval of Construction Permit, the applicant can fill the Occupancy Certificate form
by clicking Get OC.
After clicking the +New Occupancy Certificate button, the application form will show.
If Get OC is clicked then the applicant will get automatically populated fields which carry
information from CP.
If the Applicant directly apply Occupancy Certificate then all fields are empty.
Blank spaces marked with ( * ) must be filled.
Nikosh Font should be used for applying in Bengali.
In case of new owner, old owner information has to be provided by clicking on New
Owner button.
Applicant fill up the required fields and add area of floor for various uses. At first add uses
name with its area by clicking Add Uses button.
Then click Get Floor and Uses Information button. After clicking Get Floor and Uses
Information button Floor Name and Uses Descriptions are shown in the drop-down
list. Click the Add button to add it.
Fill up the all required fields and upload documents. CP related Attachments will
automatically fetched from CP Application. Rest will be uploaded by applicant.
The pay amount will autofill with VAT. The fee amount is fixed at Occupancy Certificate.
Click Submit button to submit the application
After Submission, A success message will be prompt. Click on the Close button to close the
Success message.
A SMS will also send to applicants number.
Applicant can see their files status from their account.
