Construction Permit User Guide
In any place under the jurisdiction of RAJUK, if any person or public or private organization
wants to construct a new building or infrastructure, he has to take a construction permit.
Before applying for CP,
Applicants must obtain a Land Use Clearance (LUC) for privately owned land.
After approval of Land Use Clearance, the applicant can fill the Construction Permit form
by clicking Get CP.
If the land is under RAJUK Land, Government Land, Housing Land, then NOC (No
Objection Certificate) should be taken first. Once the NOC is obtained, the applicant
can fill the construction permit form by clicking on Construction Permit on the left
side menu bar. Click on the + New Construction Permit button to apply anew.
If Get CP is clicked then the applicant will get automatically populated fields which carry
information from LUC.
Blank spaces marked with ( * ) must be filled.
Nikosh Font should be used for applying in Bengali.
To fill up the next page click Next button. Or Click Previous button to see the previous
page.
Applicant fill up the required fields and add area of floor for various uses. At first add uses
name with its area by clicking Add Uses button
Then click Get Floor and Uses Information button. After clicking Get Floor and Uses
Information button Floor Name and Uses Descriptions are shown in the drop-down
list. Click the Add button to add it.
Fill up the all required fields and upload documents. LUC related Attachments will
automatically fetched from LUC Application. Rest will be uploaded by applicant.
The pay amount will autofill with VAT. After giving the value of Total floor area of the
project (Sqm) system will automatically Calculate fees from fees Chart, Vat and , total
amount.
Click Save as Draft button to save the application
Click Submit button to submit the application
After Submission, A success message will be prompt with the Tracking Number. Click on the
Close button to close the Success message.
A SMS will also send to applicants number.
Applicant can see their files status from their account.
